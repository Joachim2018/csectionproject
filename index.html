About us

Building a voice for mothers after cesarean birth.

MED C-Section Warriors Network is a nonprofit, membership-based organization supporting women and families who experience cesarean birth. Our work includes emotional support, education, advocacy, awareness, financial empowerment, research, and partnerships.

We believe that no mother should ever be judged by how she gave birth. Every mother deserves dignity, compassion, quality healthcare, and a path to healing that is visible and supported.

Founder’s inspiration

Born from lived experience, our founder transformed painful memories into a mission of hope, healing, and systemic change for cesarean mothers worldwide.

The challenge

Many mothers heal without enough guidance or support.

From postpartum pain to emotional strain and stigma, women face a complex journey that is often misunderstood. We respond with trusted education, peer support, and compassionate advocacy.

Vision & mission

Guiding principles for whole-person healing.

Vision

A world where every mother is respected, supported, empowered, and celebrated through her cesarean journey.

Mission

To improve the quality of life of mothers who undergo cesarean births through advocacy, education, emotional wellness, partnerships, and community support.

Core values

Compassion, respect, integrity, excellence, empathy, inclusivity, accountability, collaboration, innovation, and hope.

Strategic objectives

Focused priorities that drive lasting impact.

  • Expand access to postpartum education and peer support.
  • Strengthen partnerships with hospitals, birth workers, and wellness providers.
  • Develop community-based programs for healing, advocacy, and leadership.
  • Increase funding for scholarships, volunteer training, and outreach.
